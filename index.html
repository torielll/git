How Does Brain Training Work?
1

Cognitive Skills Assessment

When it comes to your childs brain, guesswork isn’t good enough. We use cognitive skills assessments to identify the specific weaknesses that are making life harder than it needs to be.

2

Consultation

Here’s where you will receive the results of the assessment. many families describe feeling an “Aha!” moment as they see reflected on paper the weak skills responsible for some of their most frustrating daily struggles. The consultation gives families the answers they need to make informed decisions regarding interventions and solutions

3

One-on-One Brain Training

Our training uses intense mental exercise to target and strengthen weak cognitive skills. Working with a personal brain trainer makes the experience fun, while providing an effective custom workout for maximum improvements
